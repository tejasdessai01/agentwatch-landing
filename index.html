Why monitor agents?

Autonomous agents can loop indefinitely, hallucinate, or burn through API credits in minutes. AgentWatch gives you visibility and control.

Cost Tracking

Live Streaming

See what your agent is "thinking" as it happens. Stream logs directly to your dashboard.

Kill Switch

Terminate rogue agents instantly from the UI. No need to SSH into servers to kill processes.
